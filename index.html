Comprehensive User Modules

Zone Admin

  • Manage user accounts, roles, and permissions.
  • Configure stations, cabins, and track sections.

Officer / Controller

  • Review and sanction Rolling Block requirements for future dates.
  • Approve or reject Live block requests in real-time.
  • Check progress reports to analyze efficiency and productivity.

Cabin Holders (Controller & SM)

  • Acknowledge approvals and confirm field readiness.
  • Provide extension to approved blocks.
  • Reject block with appropriate reason.
  • Check the Block details thoroughly.

Field View (Supervisors)

  • Initiate Rolling Block requirements for future dates.
  • Initiate immediate Live block requests with track details.
  • Verify approvals before commencing work.
